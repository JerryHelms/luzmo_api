## Description

This "Hubspot Deal Line Items" dashboard provides a comprehensive view of a company's sales pipeline and performance. It is designed to help sales leaders, managers, and representatives track and analyze their deals throughout the sales process.

The dashboard features a variety of visualizations and filters that allow users to:

1. Monitor the overall sales performance, including the number of open deals, closed won deals, and the associated dollar amounts and annual recurring revenue (ARR).
2. Analyze deal trends by product family, product type, and stage category to identify strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities.
3. Drill down into the details of open and closed won deals, including the ability to filter by deal close date, stage, and other key attributes.

The main components of the dashboard include:

- Evolution numbers for key metrics like open deals, open amount, closed won deals, closed amount, and closed ARR.
- Dropdown filters to segment and analyze data by stage, stage category, product family, and product type.
- Bar and column charts to visualize deal totals and amounts by product type.
- Tabular views to display the details of open and closed won deals.

This dashboard would be valuable for sales leaders and managers who need to monitor the sales pipeline, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions to improve their team's performance and profitability. It provides a centralized, visual platform to track key sales metrics and uncover insights that can drive more effective sales strategies and execution.
