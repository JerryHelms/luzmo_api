## Description

This Inventory Management Dashboard Template provides a comprehensive view of a company's inventory and logistics operations. Designed for logistics and supply chain professionals, this dashboard helps track key performance indicators (KPIs) related to inventory management, product availability, and shipping times.

The dashboard includes a variety of visualizations and components that enable users to quickly identify areas for improvement and make data-driven decisions. Some of the key features and insights it provides include:

1. Inventory Levels and Stock Status: The donut charts and treemap visualization show the stock levels of different products, allowing users to easily identify which items are running low and need to be restocked.

2. Warehouse Availability: The symbol map visualizations provide a geographic overview of product availability across the company's different warehouses, helping identify any regional supply chain issues.

3. Revenue Analysis: The bubble chart and treemap visualizations break down revenue by product and product category, enabling users to focus on the most profitable items.

4. Shipping and Fulfillment: The circle gauges and column charts track the difference between real and communicated shipping times, giving insight into the company's fulfillment performance and areas for improvement.

5. Out-of-Stock Items: The regular table component lists the products that are currently out of stock, allowing the team to prioritize restocking efforts.

6. Demand Trends: The area chart visualizes the number of orders per month versus the number of out-of-stock incidents, providing valuable insights into demand patterns and the impact of inventory shortages.

Overall, this Inventory Management Dashboard Template gives logistics and supply chain teams a centralized, data-driven view of their inventory and fulfillment operations. By monitoring key KPIs and identifying problem areas, users can make more informed decisions to optimize inventory levels, improve customer service, and drive business growth.
